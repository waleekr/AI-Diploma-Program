# AIAT 116 - Ethics of Artificial Intelligence
## Python Teaching Curriculum - Master Task List

### Course Overview
**Course Code:** AIAT 116  
**Course Name:** أخلاقيات الذكاء الاصطناعي (Ethics of Artificial Intelligence)

**Course Description:**
This course delves into the ethical implications and responsibilities associated with Artificial Intelligence (AI).

**General Objective:**
This course aims to enable students to understand the ethical aspects of artificial intelligence, by analyzing issues of bias and justice, privacy protection, promoting transparency and accountability, and studying legal and regulatory frameworks to ensure responsible and fair development of artificial intelligence technologies.

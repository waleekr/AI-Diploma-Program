## Part 2: Short Answer (20 points)

### Question 6 (10 points)
Compare government-led regulation vs. industry self-regulation. What are the advantages and disadvantages of each?

**Answer:**
**Government-led regulation:**
- Advantages: Enforceable, consistent, protects public interest
- Disadvantages: May be slow, could stifle innovation, may lack technical expertise

**Industry self-regulation:**
- Advantages: Faster, industry expertise, flexible
- Disadvantages: May prioritize profit, inconsistent, less enforceable

**Best approach**: Multi-stakeholder governance combining both.

---

### Question 7 (10 points)
Describe the key components of an effective AI governance framework.

**Answer:**
1. **Clear principles**: Ethical guidelines and values
2. **Risk assessment**: Identify and categorize risks
3. **Standards and guidelines**: Technical and ethical standards
4. **Oversight mechanisms**: Ethics boards, audits, reviews
5. **Compliance**: Monitoring and enforcement
6. **Stakeholder engagement**: Multi-party involvement
7. **Transparency**: Public reporting and disclosure
8. **Continuous improvement**: Regular updates and adaptation

## Part 3: Case Study (15 points)

### Question 8 (15 points)
A company is developing AI for autonomous vehicles. Design a governance framework that addresses:
1. Regulatory compliance
2. Ethical considerations
3. Safety requirements
4. Stakeholder involvement
5. Future challenges

**Answer:**
1. **Regulatory Compliance**: Adhere to transportation regulations, safety standards, data protection laws
2. **Ethical Considerations**: Fairness in decision-making (trolley problem scenarios), transparency, accountability
3. **Safety Requirements**: Rigorous testing, fail-safe mechanisms, human oversight, continuous monitoring
4. **Stakeholder Involvement**: Government, industry, consumers, safety experts, ethicists, public input
5. **Future Challenges**: Evolving technology, new regulations, ethical dilemmas, public acceptance, liability issues

**Framework should include**: Ethics board, safety testing protocols, transparency reports, public engagement, regulatory liaison, continuous monitoring, update mechanisms.

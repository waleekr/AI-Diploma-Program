## For Students | للطلاب

### How to Use Solutions:

1. **Try First:**
   - Always attempt exercises yourself first
   - Don't look at solutions immediately
   - Struggle is part of learning!

2. **Check After:**
   - After attempting, check solutions
   - Compare your approach
   - Learn from differences

3. **Understand, Don't Copy:**
   - Understand why solutions work
   - Don't just copy code
   - Adapt solutions to your style
